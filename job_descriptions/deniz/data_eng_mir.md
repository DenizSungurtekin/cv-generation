À propos de l’offre d’emploi
Company Description

Joining Mirabaud, means entering an entrepreneurial and innovative environment. With the 7th generation of the Mirabaud family working within the bank, the culture is that of a family business. With over 750 employees based in 10 countries worldwide, Mirabaud offers opportunities to develop one’s career across an international network of 15 offices.

Job Description

Work with IT Application owners and Business Analysts to capture and define Data Hub requirements;
Actively participates with our Data Architect in the functional and technical target solution;
Participate in the evolution of our Data Hub and of its data delivery applications (Azure Synapse, Azure Logic Apps);
Analyze and resolve detected anomalies and document the work performed;
Respect the instructions and standards in place, design, perform and execute unit tests;
Organize and deliver trainings to the Application support team members;

Qualifications

Degree in computer engineering or with equivalent professional experience;
At least 5 years’ experience as a Data Engineer within a bank;
Good knowledge in the field of banking and wealth management;
Very good Knowledge of pySpark, Microsoft Azure Cloud and Synapse, Logic Apps and PureView are an Asset. 
Good knowledge of SQL, T-SQL or PL/SQL;
Mastery of computer software such as MS-Office;
Problem solver and team player with great adaptability skills;
Capable to work in French and English environment with a high capacity to handle stress.
